Welcome to "GitHub Contributions" v2.0.0!

Thanks for updating and using v1!

v2.0.0 allows you to customize GitHub's contribution view, instead of just providing the longest yearly streak.
Be sure to select your preferences!

Now, by default, the extension doesn't modify contribution views.
You get to pick and choose which information is displayed, and how.
You can read more in-depth here.

You can select your preferences under options in your Chrome Extensions tab (chrome://extensions).
Or by clicking the browser bar icon.
